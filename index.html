Build the Trio

You've got a $10M NIL budget to sign one QB, one RB, and one WR from the last 11 years of college football. Get as close as you can to the best trio ever.

1
Spin each position — QB first, then RB, then WR. Each spin deals four real players at different NIL prices. You get up to 3 spins per position, so re-spin if you don't like the board.
2
Sign one, bank the rest. The top talent is expensive — you can afford at most one true blue-chip all game. Overspend early and you're bargain-hunting the rest, and you can't see the next spin until you commit.
3
Chase the ceiling. Your total yards and TDs are measured against the best possible trio in the dataset, and your percentile is how you stack up against realistic budget builds — not random noise, so a high percentile is genuinely earned.

Unspent NIL scores you nothing. The best builds spend almost all of it — on the right players.
